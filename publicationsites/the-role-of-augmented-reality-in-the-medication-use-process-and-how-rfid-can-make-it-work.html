Augmented Reality & RFID: The Future of Sterile Preparation

Published 01/02/2023 by Barry McClain, PharmD MS

1. Demonstration: Sterile Preparation Concept Using HoloLens 2

This quick overview demonstrates how the Microsoft HoloLens 2 can be integrated into the medication use process, specifically for sterile compounding verification.

2. Shining Light on the Matter: AR & RFID Integration

This presentation explores a futuristic model that combines Augmented Reality (AR) with Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) to revolutionize sterile product preparation safety and workflow.

3. Concept Demo: AR/RFID Syringe Tracking

View GIF on Imgflip

How It Works:

  1. Dual Tags: Red dots represent two passive RFID tags: one fixed on the tip of the syringe, and one attached to the plunger.
  2. Volume Calculation: As the plunger moves to draw up medication, the distance between the two tags changes.
  3. Holographic Overlay: The system calculates the volume based on tag distance and projects this data as a hologram visible through AR hardware.
