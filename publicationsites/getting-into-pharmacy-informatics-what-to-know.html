Getting Into Pharmacy Informatics: Presentation & Resources

Published 10/16/2022 by Barry McClain, PharmD MS

Abstract

Topic: Getting Into Pharmacy Informatics: What to Know

This comprehensive guide provides an in-depth overview of pharmacy informatics, tailored for pharmacy students, practicing pharmacists, and healthcare professionals interested in the field. It explores the intersection of the medication use process and information technology, highlighting how informatics leads to safer, more efficient, and cost-effective patient outcomes.

The content covers the history of the discipline, typical daily activities—ranging from database management and clinical decision support to automation and analytics—and the essential "art and science" skills required for success. Additionally, it offers actionable advice on navigating career pathways, emphasizing the value of residency training, the importance of "soft skills" like leadership and communication, and practical steps for gaining relevant experience in the healthcare IT sector.
