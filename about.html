About Pharmacyinformatics.net

Pharmacyinformatics.net is an online platform that provides professional-level information, education, tools, and solutions for the specialty of pharmacy informatics and beyond. Although it might not look like much today, the plan is to keep creating content that is readily sharable and helpful to those improving medication use!
