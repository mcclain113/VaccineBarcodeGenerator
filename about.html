About PharmacyInformatics.net

PharmacyInformatics.net is an online platform dedicated to providing professional-level information, education, tools, and solutions for the specialty of pharmacy informatics and beyond.

Although it might not look like much today, the plan is to continuously create content that is readily sharable and helpful to those improving the medication use process!
